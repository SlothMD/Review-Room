# Product Vision

Review Author helps a reviewer turn rough notes into paste-ready Amazon review fields while keeping generation local through Ollama.

The product should preserve the reviewer's actual sentiment and voice. Product-page content is supporting context, not the source of opinion. Success means the generated title, body, and suggested stars feel like a usable first draft from a real person and require minimal cleanup before pasting.

The next product direction is to move generated output into a durable Chrome tab so the review survives popup closure and supports iteration. That workspace should let the user refine the draft with feedback, missing Amazon keyword/topic guidance, and answers to product follow-up questions. Review history and future provider flexibility should be planned, but the MVP remains Chrome-only, unpacked-folder, and Ollama-backed.
